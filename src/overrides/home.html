Built for teams

Software companies are required to adapt to rapid technological, economical, and political change in order to viably compete on any market. This fact introduces the paradox of providing a stable and low-latency experience while continuously responding to user feedback and regulatory compliance. This book discusses established strategies of nurturing a growth mindset for sustainably scaling the complexity of software products.
