A book for modern software engineering companies

The starting point for advancing our digital market competitiveness.

Our premise

Successful software companies adapt to rapid technological, economic, and political changes to viably compete in any market. This fact introduces the paradox of providing a stable and low-latency experience while continuously responding to user feedback and regulatory compliance.

Within Engineering Collaboration, we discuss a vertical slice of strategies that have proven useful for a broad spectrum of organizations. The three sections of this book provide the foundation for building an autonomous, flexible, and collaborative environment at the core of our company.

Collaborating within a Company

How do we encourage organic collaboration while limiting noise? Part I discusses strategies for cultivating a growth mindset and sustainably increasing the complexity of our product.

This section covers policies to advance the professional expertise of our employees while expanding the number of teams within our organization.

The only kind of writing is rewriting

Since the release of Engineering Collaboration, we have published two of the three parts in various levels of refinement. Focused editorial simmering on Part I and Part II is cooking their core subject matters into easily digestable workings of writing.
