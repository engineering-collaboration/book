Scale with teams

Software companies must adapt to rapid technological, economic, and political changes to viably compete in any market. This fact introduces the paradox of providing a stable and low-latency experience while continuously responding to user feedback and regulatory compliance. How do we extend current solutions to achieve continous improvements?
